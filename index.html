Using PCL Tools
8 April 2016

In our introductory post on the topic, we expressed a desire to bring together PDAL and PCL. The first step that we took nearly four years ago was to create a writer for the PCD format. Jump forward two years, and we eventually added PCL-backed drivers for both PCD reading and writing, adding support for not just ASCII data, but binary compressed data as well. With these drivers in place, we can now take the obvious first step for anybody experimenting with PDAL and PCL – just convert some data to PCD to use with PCL’s command line tools. ... Read More
